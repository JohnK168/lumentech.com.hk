Lumentech Company Ltd, established in 2010 in Hong Kong, supplies LED lighting products and solutions to the local market and overseas. High energy efficiency, long operating life and reduced maintenance provide incentives to use LED for retrofit and new building applications, which include offices, shopping malls, warehouses, hotels and residences, transport and street lighting.

John Kwan, C. Eng. MIET, founder of the Company, has extensive industrial experience in semiconductors, product safety testing and lighting fixtures, with more than 10 recent years dedicated to LED components and lighting. In addition to standard products, the Company has a network of designers and factories in place to provide system integration and customization.

For further energy saving, daylight harvesting sensors and motion sensors are used where appropriate. The Company has successfully implemented projects involving analog and digital lighting control.
